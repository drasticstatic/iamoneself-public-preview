The Teachings at I Am One Self

A Course in Miracles

The Course is a self-study spiritual thought system that teaches that the way to universal love and peace — or remembering God — is by undoing guilt through forgiving others. The 76 Miracle Principles form the cognitive backbone of our teaching.

Plant Medicine Traditions

For thousands of years, indigenous healers of the Amazon have worked with master plants — Ayahuasca, Ajo Sacha, Bobinsana, Chuchuhuasi — as sacred allies in the healing of the mind and spirit. Each plant carries a specific intelligence and correction for the ego's errors.

Plants & Miracles

Our core teaching bridges A Course in Miracles with the shamanic plant wisdom tradition. During ceremony, the sacred brew amplifies these principles so you can experience them directly — cognitive shifts from body-identification (darkness/guilt) to soul-identification (light/peace).

Spiritual Sight & the Christ-Vision

The ego's world is darkness: fear, guilt, separation. The Holy Spirit's correction is the light: love, innocence, unity. This is the shift from body-identification to soul-identification.

True Forgiveness & Innocence

True forgiveness is not pardoning someone for what they did — it is the recognition that what the ego says happened never occurred in truth. The Holy Spirit's forgiveness says: You are guiltless, because what you seemed to do in the dream never really happened.

The Golden Halo of Light

The Golden Halo represents the state of Single-Mindedness — where the ego's dualistic thought system dissolves and only the unified awareness of Spirit remains. The Barn Owl symbolizes the Eagle and Condor flying as one — two teachings reconciled in a single radiant light.

Sufi Message & The Eagle-Condor Prophecy

The Sufi Message of Love, Harmony, and Beauty is the spiritual lineage underlying this work. The prophecy says the Western mind of Medicine (The Eagle) will join with the Indigenous mind of Plant Wisdom (The Condor) for the good of humanity. The two teachings now live in harmony.
